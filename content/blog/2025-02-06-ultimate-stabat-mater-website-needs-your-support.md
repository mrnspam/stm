The Ultimate Stabat Mater Website began as a personal passion of Hans van der Velden in 1997. Now, almost 30 years later, you can listen to seven centuries of Stabat Mater music, find the text of the Latin poem in more than thirty languages, and read news updates about composers and artists inspired by the Stabat Mater.

## We Need Your Support

Our website exists purely through volunteer work and individual donations. We receive no institutional funding and are committed to remaining advertisement-free. This independence allows us to focus entirely on our mission: preserving and sharing the rich heritage of Stabat Mater music. In 2024, we reached a milestone with our book Stabat Mater Dolorosa – A Journey Through Seven Centuries of Music and launched our community forum. Every week, we discover and document new Stabat Mater compositions, enriching our collection for future generations.
